Flood Response Protocol:
1. Immediate Activation: Deploy rescue boats and high-water vehicles.
2. Evacuation: Move residents to pre-designated shelters in schools and community centers.
3. Medical: Establish mobile triages at safe zones.
4. Logistics: Ensure supply of clean water and dry rations for 48 hours.
5. Communications: Maintain satellite radio contact if cellular networks fail.

Fire Response Protocol:
1. Containment: Deploy fire tenders to incident perimeter.
2. Evacuation: Clear 500m radius of all personnel.
3. Medical: Alert nearest burn center.
4. Alerts: Issue smoke inhalation hazard notice.
